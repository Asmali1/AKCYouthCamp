About All Kids Count Youth Camp

All Kids Count Youth Camp envisions a world in which children grow up exploring and connecting with nature, thinking for themselves and acting to preserve our environment.

Why Choose AKCYC?

We go beyond ordinary camps: immersive nature experiences, personalized mentorship, and eco-friendly practices. Our holistic approach ensures every camper builds confidence, resilience, and environmental stewardship.

Camp Activities

🔥
Camping & Campfire

Pitch tents, cook over a campfire, share stories under the stars.

🥾
Hiking & Nature Walks

Guided forest treks, flora & fauna identification, wilderness survival tips.

🛶
Kayaking & Water Play

Safe paddle training, river exploration, team water games.

🎨
Arts & Crafts

Nature-inspired painting, recycled-material creations, friendship bracelet making.

⚽
Team Sports & Games

Soccer tournaments, relay races, scavenger hunts, cooperative games.

🧗‍♂️
High Ropes & Climbing

Supervised confidence-building challenges on our ropes course.
