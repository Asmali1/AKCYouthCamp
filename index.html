About All Kids Count Youth Camp

All Kids Count Youth Camp envisions a world in which children grow up exploring and connecting with nature, thinking for themselves, and acting to preserve our environment. Our mission is to foster confidence, creativity, and environmental stewardship through hands-on experiences in the great outdoors.

Why Choose AKCYC?

We go beyond ordinary camps: immersive nature experiences, personalized mentorship, and eco-friendly practices. Our holistic approach ensures every camper builds confidence, resilience, and environmental stewardship—just like the colorful spirit of All Kids Count!
